Frequently Asked Questions

How can I change my booking?

You can change your booking by visiting the “Manage Booking” section and entering your ticket number or email address.

What is the baggage allowance?

Economy passengers are allowed 1 check-in bag up to 23kg and 1 cabin bag up to 7kg. Business class allows more.

Can I cancel my flight and get a refund?

Yes, cancellation policies depend on your fare type. Refundable fares allow cancellation with a full or partial refund.

What documents are required for international travel?

A valid passport, visa (if applicable), and a confirmed return ticket are required for most destinations.
